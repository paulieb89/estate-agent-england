# Material Information Checklist

A working checklist for England residential property sales based on the DMCCA principle that
information material to a buyer's decision must be disclosed. This checklist is not legally
definitive and should be reviewed as government guidance evolves.

Each item has three states: **Confirmed** | **Unknown — needs verification** | **N/A**

## Notes on Use

This checklist is a working reference. It is not a substitute for:
- Conveyancing searches (local authority, drainage, environmental, chancel)
- A building survey or homebuyer report
- Legal title investigation
- HMRC or financial compliance review

Unknown answers on any material item should be passed to the seller to investigate before listing.
Items requiring legal or technical expertise should be referred to the seller's conveyancer, surveyor,
or other specialist.
